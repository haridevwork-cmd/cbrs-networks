About CBRS NeutralHost Services LLC

A people-first staffing firm built on integrity, expertise, and a relentless commitment to connecting talent with opportunity.

Our Story

More Than Just a Staffing Agency

Founded in 2010, CBRS NeutralHost Services LLC began with a simple belief: that great businesses are built by great people — and finding those people is the most important work any organization can do.

What started as a two-person boutique IT recruiting shop in Atlanta has grown into a national staffing powerhouse with offices in 8 cities, over 150 dedicated recruiting professionals, and more than 5,000 successful placements across every major industry.

Our success is rooted in genuine relationships — not transactions. We take time to understand what businesses truly need and what candidates genuinely want, creating matches that last and deliver lasting value on both sides.

Mission & Values

The Principles That Guide Everything We Do

Our core values aren't just words on a wall — they shape every decision, interaction, and placement we make.

Integrity First

We operate with complete transparency and honesty — with clients, candidates, and each other. Trust is earned through consistent actions.

Excellence in Execution

We hold ourselves to the highest standards. Average results are not acceptable — we pursue exceptional outcomes for everyone we serve.

People-Centered

Behind every job order is a human story. We treat every candidate with respect and dignity throughout their entire journey with us.

Innovation & Agility

The world of work changes fast. We embrace technology and new approaches to stay ahead and deliver better results for our partners.

Diversity & Inclusion

We believe diverse teams outperform. We actively build inclusive candidate slates and partner with clients who share this commitment.

Community Impact

Great careers change lives. We invest in workforce development programs and community partnerships that create opportunity for all.

Our Journey

Key Milestones in Our Growth

2010 – Founded

CBRS NeutralHost Services LLC launched in Atlanta, GA, focused exclusively on IT recruiting for mid-size technology companies.

2013 – Expanded to Healthcare

Launched our Healthcare division, growing the team to 30 recruiters and opening our second office in Nashville, TN.

2016 – National Footprint

Reached 1,000+ placements and opened offices in Dallas, Chicago, and New York, serving Fortune 500 clients nationally.

2020 – Digital Transformation

Invested heavily in AI-powered candidate matching and launched our proprietary TalentLink™ platform for clients.

2026 – Industry Leaders

5,000+ placements, 150+ staff, 8 offices, and recognized as a Top 10 staffing firm by Staffing Industry Analysts.
